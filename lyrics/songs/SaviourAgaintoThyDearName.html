English

VERSE1
Savior, again to Thy dear Name we raise With one accord our parting hymn of praise; We stand to bless Thee ere our worship cease; Then, lowly kneeling, wait Thy word of peace.

VERSE2
Grant us Thy peace upon our homeward way; With Thee began, with Thee shall end the day. Guard thou the lips from sin, the hearts from shame, That in this house have called upon Thy Name.

VERSE3
Grant us Thy peace, through this approaching night; Turn Thou for us its darkness into light; From harm and danger, fear and shame kept free, For dark and light are both alike to Thee.

VERSE4
Grant us Thy peace throughout our earthly life; Our balm in sorrow, and our stay in strife; Then, when thy voice shall bid our conflict cease, Call us, O Lord, to Thine eternal peace.
